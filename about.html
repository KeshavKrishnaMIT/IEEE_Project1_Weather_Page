About This Page

This Weather App allows you to check the current weather of any city in the world with a lot more details.

Start typing a city/country name in the search box and click Search to see the weather details.

You’ll also get fun and helpful suggestions based on the current weather — perfect for planning your day!

Data is fetched using the OpenWeatherMap API.
